How to Play Poker

Basics of Poker

Poker is a game of skill, strategy, and luck. The goal is to win chips by creating the best hand or convincing your opponents to fold.

Hand Rankings

Royal Flush

A, K, Q, J, 10, all of the same suit.

Straight Flush

Five consecutive cards of the same suit.

Four of a Kind

Four cards of the same rank.

Full House

Three of a kind and a pair.

Gameplay

In each round, players are dealt two cards, and five community cards are placed on the table. Players use these cards to make the best possible five-card hand.

Betting Rounds

There are four betting rounds in Texas Hold'em: Pre-Flop, Flop, Turn, and River. Players can call, raise, or fold based on their hands and strategies.
