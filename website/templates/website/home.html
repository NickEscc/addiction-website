How to Play

In poker, the objective is to make the best hand possible using a combination of cards. Here are some basic steps:

  1. Each player is dealt two private cards (known as hole cards).
  2. The dealer places five community cards on the table (called the "board").
  3. Players can use these cards to create the best five-card hand.
  4. The player with the best hand at the end of the round wins the pot.
